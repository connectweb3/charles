Introduction

The Citadel of Charles platformer game is the flagship title in our ecosystem, blending classic 2D platforming with blockchain rewards. Players navigate memecoin-inspired worlds, collecting $COC tokens and NFTs while battling challenges in a vibrant, futuristic universe.

Gameplay Mechanics

Core mechanics include fluid platforming, power-ups, and boss battles. Controls are intuitive: arrow keys for movement, space for jump, and action buttons for interactions. Levels are procedurally generated with memecoin themes, ensuring replayability.

In-Game Economy

Earn $COC through gameplay milestones, daily quests, and PvP arenas. Integrate with staking for boosted rewards. All transactions are on-chain, providing true ownership of in-game assets.

  • Token Rewards: Up to 100 $COC per level completion
  • NFT Drops: Rare collectibles from boss defeats
  • Marketplace: Trade items peer-to-peer

Key Features

- Multiplayer Co-op Modes

- Customizable Avatars with NFT Integration

- Dynamic Soundtrack and Visual Effects

- Cross-Platform Play (Web, Mobile)

Blockchain Integration

Powered by Solana for seamless transactions. Wallet connect via Phantom or Solflare. All earnings are withdrawable to your personal wallet, bridging fun and finance.
